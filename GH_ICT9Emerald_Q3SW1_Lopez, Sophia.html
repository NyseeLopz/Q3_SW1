Synopsis

From the creative comic book world of Alice Oseman, the series follow two teenagers--Nick and Charlie--as they navigate their identity and their love for each other.

Characters
Charlie Spring
 A 17 year old boy who's quite high-strung and tends to overthink things. He's also the boyfriend of Nick Nelson.
Nick Nelson
 A rugby player known for his sweet and kind demeanor towards everyone. His romantic feelings for Charlie grow throughout the series.
Elle Argent
 A transfer student from Truham Grammar School for Boys to Harvey Greene Grammar School for Girls, she's a very caring individual despite being bullied for her transition.
Tao Xu
 As one of the most protective friends of Charlie's friend group, he's distrustful of Nick as he thinks that Charlie could be taken advantage of.
Tori Spring
 A pessimistic, cynical, and introverted teenager who is very caring of Charlie, her baby brother. She often appears in the series every now and then with her "older sister magic."
